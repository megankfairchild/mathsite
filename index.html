Service

Math Empowerment Group

Creator and organizer of the Math Empowerment Group at the University of Utah.
The Math Empowerment Group offers a welcoming environment for students who feel overwhelmed or anxious about math. Through peer connection, guided discussions, and low-pressure activities, students can explore strategies to build confidence and reduce stress. Our main goals are to build a more positive relationship with math, encourage growth, build a sense of community, and support confidence-building across all levels of math ability. We embrace students of all backgrounds and math experiences.
