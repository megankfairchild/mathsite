Intro

Welcome to my personal webpage and thank you for visiting!

I just finished my fourth year of the doctoral program in mathematics at LSU being co-advised by Oliver Dasbach and Shea Vela-Vick. I plan to graduate in May 2025. My career goal is to stay in academia, hopefully continuing research and teaching mathematics at the university level.

Tutoring

I have been tutoring mathematics since I was in high school, where I was also a classroom assistant. I have tutored math from the high school level, private tutored everything from graduate topology to college algebra, and worked in the mathematics tutoring lab at Texas State.
Helping others learn and understand mathematics has always been a passion of mine. A fun fact, I have wanted to be a math teacher since I was in the third grade!
